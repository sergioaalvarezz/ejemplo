Álvaro Díaz is a Puerto Rican singer and rapper who mixes trap, hip hop, and reggaeton with alternative influences. He is known for his creative lyrics and introspective themes, such as love and urban life. His album "Felicilandia" (2021) consolidated him in the Latin urban music scene, and he has collaborated with artists such as C. Tangana and Rauw Alejandro. Díaz also stands out for his visual approach in his music and his artistic aesthetic.
