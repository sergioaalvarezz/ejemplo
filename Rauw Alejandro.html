Rauw Alejandro, whose real name is Raúl Alejandro Ocasio Ruiz, is a Puerto Rican singer, songwriter, and model, born on January 10, 1993 in San Juan, Puerto Rico. He is known for his musical style that combines reggaeton, pop, and R&B. He began his career in 2014 but gained international recognition with his single "Tattoo" in 2020, which was a huge hit on streaming platforms. His album "Afrodisíaco," released in 2021, consolidated his career and featured a variety of collaborations with renowned artists, which expanded his popularity. Rauw Alejandro is known for his melodic voice and lyrics that often address themes of love and relationships. He has collaborated with several prominent artists, including Rosalía, Wisin y Yandel, and has been nominated for multiple awards in the music industry. His unique style and ability to innovate within the genre have established him as one of the most influential artists of his generation in contemporary Latin music.
