Mon Laferte, whose real name is Norma Monserrat Bustamante Laferte, is a Chilean singer, songwriter, and actress, born on May 2, 1983 in Viña del Mar, Chile. She is known for her powerful voice and eclectic musical style, which spans genres such as pop, rock, bolero, and traditional Latin American music. She began her musical career in Chile, where she gained popularity with her album "Mon Laferte Vol. 1" in 2015, which included hits such as "Tu Falta de Querer." Since then, she has released several acclaimed albums, including "La Trenza" and "Norma," and has received numerous awards, including several Latin Grammys. Laferte is recognized not only for her musical talent, but also for her stage presence and personal style, which combines fashion and artistic expression. Additionally, Mon Laferte is known for her social commitment and for addressing issues of justice and equity in her lyrics and public life.
