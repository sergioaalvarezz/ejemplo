Paulo Londra

"Te boté de mi vida, pero no de mi mente"

Paulo Londra is an Argentine singer and songwriter, born on April 12, 1998 in Córdoba, Argentina. He is known for his style in the urban genre, especially in trap and reggaeton. Londra began his musical career in 2017, publishing his first songs on digital platforms and quickly gaining popularity. His single "Nena Maldición," in collaboration with Lucho SSJ, catapulted him to fame and became a huge hit in Latin America. Since then, he has released several hits, such as "Adán y Eva," "Tal Vez," and "Por Siempre." His music is characterized by lyrics that address themes of love, heartbreak, and everyday life, with a fresh and authentic approach. Paulo Londra has collaborated with several prominent artists in the industry, such as Ed Sheeran and J Balvin. Throughout his career, he has received multiple awards and recognitions, consolidating himself as one of the most promising artists in the urban music scene. His innovative style and ability to connect with his audience have made him a reference in Latin music.
