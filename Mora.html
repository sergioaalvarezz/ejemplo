Mora

"No me digas que no, porque te quiero aquí"

Mora, whose real name is Moraíto, is a Puerto Rican singer and songwriter, born on February 24, 1999. He has excelled in urban music, especially in the reggaeton and pop genres, and has gained popularity in the Latin music scene for his fresh style and emotional lyrics. Mora began his career in 2019 and quickly gained attention with his single "Como Yo Te Quiero," which resonated with young audiences. He has released several successful projects, including his albums "Microdosis" and "Antes de Morir," which have been well received by critics and the public. His music is characterized by lyrics that address themes of love, heartbreak, and everyday life, with a sensitivity that connects with his audience. He has established himself as one of the most promising emerging voices in contemporary Latin music. His ability to tell stories through his songs and his charisma have made him an influential artist in his genre.
