Junior H

"Lo que no me mata, me hace más fuerte"

Junior H, whose real name is Juan Carlos González, is a Mexican singer and songwriter, born on March 23, 2001 in Guanajuato, Mexico. He is known for his style in the regional Mexican music genre, in the subgenre of corridos tumbados. He rose to fame with songs such as "No Me Mientes" and "El Amor Es Una Moda," which have resonated with a young audience due to their emotional and authentic lyrics, which address topics such as love, heartbreak, and everyday life. Junior H has quickly gained popularity on streaming platforms and social media. His distinctive style and ability to tell stories through his lyrics have established him as one of the most relevant emerging figures in contemporary Mexican music.
