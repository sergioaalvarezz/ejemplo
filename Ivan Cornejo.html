Ivan Cornejo

"Todos tenemos un lado de nosotros que ocultamos y no lo vemos hasta después de invertir tanto tiempo"

Iván Cornejo is a Peruvian singer and songwriter, known for his style in the urban and pop music genre. He was born on March 22, 2003 in Lima, Peru. He began his musical career on social media and streaming platforms, where he quickly gained popularity due to his talent and his emotional lyrics that address themes such as love and heartbreak. He rose to fame with his single "Tú," which became a viral hit, and has released other popular songs such as "Me Duele" and "Cuando Te Veo." His music is characterized by catchy melodies and heartfelt lyrics, which has led him to be considered one of the most promising emerging artists in Latin music. Iván Cornejo has been recognized for his connection with a young audience and for his ability to create songs that resonate with their emotional experiences.
