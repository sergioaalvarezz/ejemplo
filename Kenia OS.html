Kenia OS

"No hay nada imposible si te lo propones"

Kenia Os, whose full name is Kenia Guadalupe Flores Osuna, is a Mexican influencer, singer, and content creator, born on July 15, 1999 in Mazatlán, Sinaloa. She began her career on social media, especially on YouTube, where she stood out for her vlogs, challenges, and lifestyle content, amassing millions of followers. Kenia Os has released several hit singles, including "Bonita," "La Noche," and "Te Amo" in 2021. Her musical style spans genres such as pop and urban, and her lyrics often deal with themes of love, empowerment, and self-acceptance. Her influence on social media and her success in the music industry have established her as one of the most prominent emerging figures in Mexican pop culture.
