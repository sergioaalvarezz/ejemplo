Peso Pluma

"Dame tu mano y te haré volar"

Peso Pluma, whose real name is Hassan Emilio Kabande Laija, is a Mexican singer and songwriter, born on June 15, 1999 in Guadalajara, Jalisco. He is known for his unique style that combines traditional corridos with influences from trap and reggaeton, which has positioned him as one of the most prominent artists of the new wave of Mexican urban music. Peso Pluma gained notoriety with the release of his single "Ella Baila Sola," in collaboration with Eslabón Armado, which became a huge success on streaming platforms and social media. Since then, he has released several hits, such as "La Bebé" and "Por Las Noches," consolidating his place in the music industry. His music is characterized by lyrics that address themes of love, heartbreak, and everyday life, resonating with a young audience. His fresh style and stage presence have made him an influential figure in contemporary Latin music.
