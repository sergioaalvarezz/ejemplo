Bad Bunny, whose real name is Benito Antonio Martínez Ocasio, is a Puerto Rican singer, songwriter, and rapper, born on March 10, 1994 in Vega Baja, Puerto Rico. He has become one of the most influential artists in Latin music and an icon of the urban genre. His innovative style fuses reggaeton, trap, hip-hop, and pop. Bad Bunny rose to fame with his single "Soy Peor" in 2017, and has since released several successful albums, including "X 100PRE", "YHLQMDLG", and "Un Verano Sin Ti". His lyrics address a variety of topics, from love and heartbreak to social criticism. Bad Bunny has collaborated with numerous artists, and has received multiple awards, including several Latin Grammys. In addition to his music, he is known for his unique visual style and focus on fashion, which has made him an influential figure beyond music. His impact on pop culture and ability to connect with a global audience have solidified his place as one of the most important artists of his generation.
