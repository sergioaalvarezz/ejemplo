Carla Morrison

"Eres tú, sólo tú, el que despierta en mi alma..."

Carla Morrison is a Mexican singer, songwriter and musician, born on July 19, 1986 in Tecate, Baja California. She is known for her intimate and emotional style, which mixes genres such as indie pop and folk. Her songs stand out for their deep lyrics, which address themes such as love, heartbreak and emotional vulnerability. Morrison has released successful albums such as "Déjenme Llorar" (2012), which catapulted her to fame, and "Amor Supremo" (2015). She has won several awards, including Latin Grammys, and is recognized for her soft voice and ability to convey raw emotions. Throughout her career, she has collaborated with international artists and has been one of the most influential figures in alternative music in Spanish.
