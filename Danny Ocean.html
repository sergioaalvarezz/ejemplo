Danny Ocean

"Y seguro que aun estoy aprendiendo, que mi felicidad es primero"

Danny Ocean, whose real name is Daniel Alejandro Morales, is a Venezuelan singer and songwriter born on November 5, 1992 in Caracas. He is known for his style of pop and urban music, fusing genres such as reggaeton and R&B. He rose to fame with his hit "Me Rehúso" in 2017, which became a viral phenomenon on streaming platforms and social media. Since then, Danny Ocean has released several successful singles, such as "Fuera de Mercado" and "Dembow", consolidating himself as a prominent figure in Latin music. His fresh and authentic style, coupled with his ability to write emotional lyrics, have made him an influential artist in the contemporary music scene.
